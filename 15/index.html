Project Proposal

For my final project, I don't have a full picture yet of what I want to create. Living on Harvard Yard this semester, I really want my final project to be something that I can take around and show my friends. I want my project to be accessible, so that anybody would think it was really cool without having to know how it works. Here are some ideas that I have been thinking about:

1. a tiny portable charger that attaches somehow to the back of your phone (wireless)/ small sheet or something that attaches to your phone to extend the wireless charging through a case (like a wireless friendly case converter)
2. a robot to get me something of my choice from the fridge
3. face id glasses
4. robot to make your bed
5. massage shirt/wearable for my achy back
6. autofocusing binocular glasses (follows your eyeline and focuses a zoomed picture of that object
7. light-up bluetooth sticker tracker with an app (stick it on something and will light up when activated by app)
8. a water bottle humidifier (a device you can stick inside a water bottle to humidify the room
